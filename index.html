Elektrik Eel Festival

Elektrik Eel Festival is a three-day celebration of cutting-edge live music taking place at the Royal Park in Melbourne, Australia. Featuring over 120 artists across 12 stages, this is the ultimate event for music lovers looking to immerse themselves in an electrifying aural universe.
